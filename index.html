Astrocartography
Where each planet's lines fall across the Earth
How to Read This

Each planet casts four lines across the Earth at the moment of your birth — where it was rising (AC), culminating (MC), setting (DC), and at the nadir (IC). Living near or visiting these lines activates that planet's energy in your life. MC/IC lines run north-south (constant longitude). AC/DC lines curve with latitude.
